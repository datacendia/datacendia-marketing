Frequently Asked Questions

Do you require copying our data to your systems?
No. For Private Cloud, On-Prem, and Air-Gapped deployments, your data never leaves your infrastructure.
Do you train models on customer data?
Never. Your data is used only for your analysis. We do not use customer data for model training, fine-tuning, or any purpose beyond delivering your results.
What environments do you support?
Private Cloud (your VPC), On-Premises, and fully Air-Gapped. Each has different setup times and operational requirements—we help you choose based on your security posture.
What does an auditor actually receive?
A complete evidence packet: decision summary (PDF), full decision trace (inputs, evidence, tool calls, policies applied, rationale), data lineage, agent dissent records, timestamps, and cryptographic signatures. Signed with customer-owned key (KMS/HSM) • Algorithm: Ed25519/RSA-PSS • Includes manifest.json + hashes. All exportable in JSON for programmatic verification.
What happens if we decide not to proceed?
You keep all artifacts generated during the pilot. No lock-in, no penalties.
Is there a pilot fee?
Yes. The 90-Day Pilot is $50,000 fixed fee. This covers setup, configuration, weekly syncs, and dedicated support. The fee is fully credited toward your first annual contract if you proceed.
