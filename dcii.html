Crisis Immunization

The 9 primitives that prevent institutional failure. Not reactive compliance. Proactive immunization.

Most governance frameworks treat crises as inevitable — then scramble to respond. Crisis Immunization inverts this. It builds structural antibodies into your decision-making process: proof of when decisions were made, complete capture of the reasoning behind them, accountability when humans override AI recommendations, institutional memory that survives leadership turnover, and detection of policy drift before it becomes a scandal.

Every primitive maps to a dimension of the Institutional Immune System Score (IISS) — a standardized 0–1000 score that quantifies an organization's resilience to decision crises.

Multi-Provider TSA

Batch Timestamping

CendiaQuantumKMS™

Quantum-Resistant Cryptographic Integrity

Today's RSA and ECDSA signatures will be breakable by quantum computers. Decisions signed today may need to be verified in 2040. CendiaQuantumKMS™ ensures evidence integrity survives the quantum transition by implementing NIST-standardized post-quantum algorithms alongside classical cryptography.

Post-Quantum Algorithms
NIST-standardized Dilithium (ML-DSA), SPHINCS+ (SLH-DSA), and Falcon for digital signatures. NIST security levels 1–5.
Hybrid Signatures
Dual RSA-4096 + Dilithium signing during the transition period. If either algorithm breaks, the other still protects evidence integrity.
Key Rotation & Migration
Automated key rotation with signature chain-of-custody. Migrate existing evidence to post-quantum signatures before quantum computers arrive.
HSM Integration
Hardware Security Module support for key generation and signing. Customer-controlled keys — Datacendia never holds your private keys.
