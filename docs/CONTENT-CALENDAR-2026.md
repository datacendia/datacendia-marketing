## Distribution Strategy

1. **Publish** article on /learn/{slug}/
2. **Email** monthly digest to subscriber list (1st of month)
3. **LinkedIn** post with article hook + link (Stuart's personal + company page)
4. **Repurpose** crisis articles as LinkedIn carousels
5. **Cross-link** from relevant product pages (e.g., trust.html links to compliance articles)

## Content Template

Each article should follow:
- **Hook** (1 paragraph — the problem or question)
- **Context** (regulatory background or crisis timeline)
- **Analysis** (map to DCII primitives or platform capabilities)
- **What Datacendia Does** (product tie-in, not hard sell)
- **CTA** (Request Briefing / Start Pilot / Read Related)
- **~1,500-2,000 words** for SEO depth
