Audit Briefing — Key Findings

CendiaChronos™ has reconstructed 12 months of organizational history for Nexus Health Systems. The incoming CCO's audit reveals the following:

  • 4,382 decisions on immutable ledger — every decision is cryptographically signed and tamper-proof. No gaps detected in the decision record.
  • 3 pivotal moments identified: The Series C term sheet (May 2026), the FedRAMP re-authorization (June 2026), and the cloud cost overrun (June 2026) were the decisions with the highest downstream impact.

  • SOX compliance audit passed — all financial controls verified. Wage-to-revenue ratio healthy at current headcount.
  • Cloud cost overrun (15%) flagged as the only negative financial event — root cause traced to unoptimized engineering workloads post-expansion. Recommendation: implement FinOps controls.
  • Runway: 18.0 months — up 3.1 months from last quarter. Financial position is stable and improving.
