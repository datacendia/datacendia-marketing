📋 Scenario Briefing

Greenfield Athletic FC has identified the target striker, a 24-year-old Premier League striker, as the centrepiece of their Champions League ambitions. The £65M transfer fee would be the club’s largest ever — and the £180K/week wages would make him their highest-paid player by a significant margin.

The board is split. The sporting director is convinced the player is a generational talent at a fair price. The CFO warns that the deal pushes UEFA Financial Fair Play compliance to within 2% of the breach threshold. One more signing this window becomes impossible. The club has activated Datacendia’s Sports Governance Council to deliberate the transfer from every angle — FFP compliance, sporting risk, financial impact, and governance review.
