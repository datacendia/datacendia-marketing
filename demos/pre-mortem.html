📋 Scenario Briefing

The deal has board approval, but the CEO remembers the last acquisition that "looked perfect on paper" and failed within 18 months.

Before signing, she's activated the Pre-Mortem Engine — which starts from a deliberately horrifying premise: "It is January 2027. The TechStart acquisition has failed catastrophically." The engine then works backward to map every path that could lead to that outcome, complete with failure chains, early warning signals, and prevention strategies.
