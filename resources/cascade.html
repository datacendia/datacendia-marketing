CendiaCascade™

The Butterfly Effect Engine. Predict how small changes propagate through your organization before committing resources.

The Problem: Hidden Consequences

Every enterprise decision creates ripples. A pricing change affects sales, which affects inventory, which affects supplier relationships, which affects cash flow. Traditional planning tools show you the first step. CendiaCascade shows you the entire cascade.

How It Works

  1. Input Your Change: "Reduce headcount by 15%" or "Launch premium tier at $499/mo"
  2. Watch the Ripples: Traces impacts through people, processes, systems, finances
  3. See Hidden Triggers: Identify cascade breakpoints where small effects amplify
  4. Simulate Interventions: Test mitigation strategies before the cascade happens

Cascade Depth

  • 1st Order: Layoff → Reduced payroll ✅
  • 2nd Order: → Knowledge loss → Project delays ✅
  • 3rd Order: → Customer churn → Revenue drop ✅
  • 4th Order: → Talent market signal → Harder hiring ✅
  • 5th Order: → Competitor advantage → Market share loss ✅

Traditional tools stop at 1st order. We go to 5th.
