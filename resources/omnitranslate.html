CendiaOmniTranslate™

Enterprise translation across 100+ languages. AI-powered. Runs entirely on your infrastructure.

Why Sovereign Translation Matters

Most enterprise translation sends your sensitive documents to third-party cloud APIs. CendiaOmniTranslate runs on YOUR infrastructure—your data never leaves your network.

Capabilities

  • 100+ Languages: From major languages to low-resource languages with enhanced prompting
  • RTL Support: Full support for Arabic, Hebrew, Urdu, Persian, and other RTL languages
  • Enterprise Glossary: Custom terminology that stays consistent across all translations
  • Translation Memory: Reuse previous translations for consistency and speed
  • Document Translation: Translate Council decisions, audit reports, summaries
  • Auto Language Detection: Automatically detect source language
  • Batch Processing: Translate thousands of documents in parallel

Language Tiers

  • Tier 1 (Fast): English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Dutch, Russian, Chinese, Japanese, Korean, Arabic
  • Tier 2 (Standard): Polish, Ukrainian, Thai, Vietnamese, Indonesian, Malay, Tagalog, Hindi, Bengali, Turkish, Hebrew, Persian, Swedish, Danish
  • Tier 3 (Enhanced): Low-resource languages with specialized prompting for accuracy

Technical Stack

  • Primary Model: Qwen 2.5 32B (dedicated for translation)
  • Fallback Model: Qwen 2.5 14B
  • Fast Model: Qwen 2.5 7B for Tier 1 languages
  • Infrastructure: Ollama-based, runs on your hardware
