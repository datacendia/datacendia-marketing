Privacy

How we handle data on this website and in our platform.

Website Analytics

This marketing website does not use cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party analytics. We do not collect personal data through this site except when you explicitly submit it via the briefing request form.

Briefing Request Form

When you submit a briefing request, we collect:

  • Name, title, and organization
  • Email address
  • Security environment preference
  • Optional briefing context

This information is used solely to schedule and prepare your technical briefing. We do not share this data with third parties or use it for marketing purposes beyond your requested briefing.

Platform Data Handling

Datacendia's platform is designed for data sovereignty. Depending on your deployment model:

  • Air-Gapped / On-Prem: Your data never leaves your infrastructure. We have zero access.
  • Private Cloud (VPC): Data remains in your cloud environment. We access only metadata for support when explicitly authorized.
  • Cloud (SaaS): Data is encrypted at rest and in transit. Access is logged and auditable. You can export and delete at any time.
