## 1. THE COUNCIL — Multi-Agent Deliberation

### What It Is
Multiple specialized AI agents debate a decision before any recommendation is made. Each agent has a defined role, cites evidence, and can dissent.

### Who Needs It
Every organization that makes decisions with consequences.

### The Pitch (30 seconds)
> "Right now, your CEO asks for advice and gets one answer from one person's perspective. With The Council, they get five competing viewpoints — risk, finance, operations, legal, ethics — all arguing their case with evidence. The full debate is recorded. The decision is signed. And if it goes wrong, there's a complete record of what was considered."

### Deployment Checklist
- [ ] Define 3-5 initial agent roles relevant to the organization
- [ ] Select first decision topic (recommend: a pending strategic decision)
- [ ] Configure compliance frameworks relevant to jurisdiction
- [ ] Set up human reviewer (who signs off on the final decision)
- [ ] Run first deliberation and review Decision Packet

### ROI Calculation
- **Time saved:** 8-16 hours per strategic decision (no more scheduling 5 senior leaders for a 2-hour meeting)
- **Risk avoided:** Quantify the cost of the last bad decision that lacked diverse perspectives
- **Compliance value:** Audit preparation time reduced by 80%
