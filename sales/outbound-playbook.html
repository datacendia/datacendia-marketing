🚀 Key Product Capabilities

Use these in conversations to differentiate from generic AI tools

The Council

14 specialized AI agents that debate every decision from different perspectives — risk, legal, financial, operational, ethical. Not a single model guessing — a structured deliberation.

"Instead of one AI giving you an answer, you get a boardroom debate with documented dissent."

Decision DNA

One-click export of complete audit artifacts — PDF + JSON bundles with Merkle tree integrity verification. Cryptographically signed. Regulator-ready.

"When the auditor asks 'show me why you decided this' — one click, done."

CendiaDissent

Formal dissent filing system with retaliation protection. When an agent (or human) disagrees with a decision, it's logged permanently. Outcome verification tracks if dissenters were right.

"Your best people's warnings won't get buried in email anymore."

CendiaCascade

The Butterfly Effect engine. Maps how small changes propagate through your organization — second and third-order consequences before you commit.

"See what breaks downstream before you make the change."

Deterministic Replay

Bit-perfect reproducibility. Re-run any past decision with pinned random seeds and captured state. Prove the same inputs produce the same outputs.

"Run it again two years later — get the exact same result. That's what regulators want."

TPM Attestation

Hardware-signed decisions using Trusted Platform Module. Not just software signatures — the silicon itself vouches for the decision integrity.

"Hardware-level proof that no one tampered with the decision after the fact."

QR Air-Gap Bridge

Animated QR code sequences for zero-media data transfer. Get data into air-gapped environments without USB drives or network connections.

"For environments where even a USB drive is a security risk."

100+ Language Support

CendiaOmniTranslate handles enterprise translation with glossary management and translation memory. Decisions documented in any language your stakeholders need.

"Your Tokyo office reads the decision in Japanese. Your Berlin office in German. Same audit trail."

📞 Discovery Questions

  • Qualification: "How many strategic decisions does your leadership team make per quarter? M&A, major investments, product launches?"
  • Pain discovery: "When a decision is questioned later — by regulators, board, or lawyers — how do you reconstruct the rationale?"
  • Current state: "What tools do you use today for decision support? BI tools? Consultants? Internal analysts?"
  • Budget: "What do you spend annually on consulting for strategic analysis?"
  • Sovereignty: "Are there data residency or cloud restrictions that limit what AI tools you can use?"
  • Dissent capture: "When someone on your team disagrees with a decision, how is that documented? Does it get lost?"
  • Consequence analysis: "Before major decisions, do you map out second and third-order effects across departments?"
  • Urgency: "Have you had a situation where you wished you had better documentation of a past decision?"
  • Champion: "Who else would need to be involved in evaluating something like this?"
